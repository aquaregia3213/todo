## **AI Life OS** 

## **Tagline** 

Your personal career, learning, and growth operating system. 

## **Problem** 

Students are overwhelmed by information. 

They don't know: 

- Which skills to learn 

- Which career path to choose 

- How to get internships 

- What projects to build 

- How to improve their resume 

- What to do every day 

Current AI assistants answer questions. 

They do not create long-term execution plans. 

## **Solution** 

AI Life OS converts goals into action plans. 

Users describe: 

- Education 

- Skills 

- Interests 

- Budget 

- Available time 

- Career goals 

The system generates: 

- Personalized roadmap 

- Daily tasks 

- Weekly goals 

- Project recommendations 

1 

- Learning resources 

- Internship strategy 

## **Example** 

Input: 

"I am a second-year mechanical engineering student. I know basic C++. I have ₹5000 budget. I want a software internship." 

Output: 

- 30-day roadmap 

- 90-day roadmap 

- 6-month roadmap 

- Recommended courses 

- Recommended projects 

- Daily tasks 

## **Target Audience** 

Primary: 

- Engineering students 

- Diploma students 

- Fresh graduates 

Secondary: 

- Career switchers 

- Self learners 

## **Core Principle** 

Every response must contain: 

1. Current situation 

2. Recommended path 

3. Next action 

4. Expected outcome 

5. Timeline 

The AI should guide execution, not just provide information. 

2 

## **Long-Term Vision** 

Become the default personal growth assistant for students and young professionals in India. 

Users should open AI Life OS daily and know exactly what to work on next.
